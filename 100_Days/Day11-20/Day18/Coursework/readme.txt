### --- Exercises --- ###

Todays exercises are a little different, instead of being standalone code pieces they are a series
of challenges dedicated to testing our knowlege at turtle. As such instead of seperate files, will
include them all under the one file. challenges.py

Challenge List:

Using turtles documentation get turtle to do the following

1/ Draw a square
2/ Draw a dashed line
3/ Draw multiple different shapes on top of each other
4/ Generate a random walk
5/ Draw a spirograph
